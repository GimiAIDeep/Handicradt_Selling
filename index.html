About ArtisanHub

Empowering Artisans, Preserving Heritage

ArtisanHub is a revolutionary platform dedicated to preserving India's rich handicraft heritage while providing sustainable livelihoods for traditional artisans. We bridge the gap between skilled craftsmen and conscious buyers who value authenticity and craftsmanship.

Our mission is to create a fair-trade ecosystem where artisans receive proper recognition and compensation for their exquisite work, while buyers gain access to genuine, high-quality handicrafts directly from the creators.

With over 5,000 registered artisans from across India, we represent the diversity of Indian handicrafts - from Kashmir's Pashmina to Kerala's coir products, from Rajasthan's blue pottery to Assam's bamboo crafts.

How ArtisanHub Works

Artisan Registration

Artisans create their profile by providing details about their craft, experience, and uploading samples of their work. Our team verifies each artisan to ensure authenticity.

1

Product Listing

Verified artisans list their products with detailed descriptions, high-quality images, and pricing. They can choose to sell ready-made items or take custom orders.

2

Buyer Discovery

Buyers browse through our curated collections, search by craft type or region, and discover unique handicrafts. Each product page includes the artisan's story.

3

Secure Transaction

Buyers place orders through our secure payment gateway. Funds are held in escrow until delivery confirmation to protect both buyers and artisans.

4

Shipping & Delivery

Artisans package their creations with care, and our logistics partners ensure safe delivery across India. Buyers receive tracking updates.

5

Feedback & Support

After delivery, buyers can leave reviews and ratings. Our support team is always available to assist with any queries or concerns.

6
